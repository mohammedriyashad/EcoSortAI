WET WASTE:
Includes food scraps, vegetable peels, tea bags, leftover food.
Disposal: Compost bin or wet waste bin.

DRY WASTE:
Includes paper, plastic, metal, glass, cardboard.
Disposal: Clean and put into dry waste bin. Avoid mixing with wet waste.

HAZARDOUS WASTE:
Includes batteries, expired medicines, chemicals, paint containers.
Disposal: Must be stored separately and given to municipal hazardous waste collection.

E-WASTE:
Includes phones, chargers, laptops, cables, electronic boards.
Disposal: Only via authorized e-waste recyclers or collection drives.

GENERAL RULE:
Never mix hazardous or e-waste with wet waste. This contaminates landfill and creates pollution.
